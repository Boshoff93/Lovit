# App Showcase

Create promotional videos from app screenshots.

## Overview

App Showcase transforms your app screenshots into engaging promotional videos - perfect for app store previews, social media ads, and product launches.

## How It Works

1. **Upload Screenshots** - Capture your app's key screens
2. **Create App Asset** - Gruvi builds a reference from your screenshots
3. **Generate Video** - AI creates smooth transitions and animations
4. **Add Audio** - Include music or voiceover
5. **Publish** - Share to app stores and social platforms

## Token Cost

**50 tokens per second** of video (same as other video types)

## Creating Your App Asset

### Screenshot Requirements

**Quantity:** 5-10 screenshots recommended

**What to capture:**
- Splash/Login screen
- Main dashboard/home
- Key feature screens
- Settings or profile
- Any "wow" moments

**Quality tips:**
- Use device frames (optional but professional)
- Consistent device orientation
- High resolution (screenshot from device, not web)
- Remove any test/debug data

### Upload Process

1. Go to AI Assets → App/Software
2. Upload all screenshots
3. Name your app asset
4. Gruvi processes and creates the asset

## Video Styles

### Product Demo
Clean, professional walkthrough of features
- Best for: App Store previews, investor decks

### Feature Highlight
Focus on one specific capability
- Best for: Social media, feature launches

### Animated Tour
Fun, dynamic showcase with motion
- Best for: TikTok/Reels, brand awareness

### Tutorial Style
Step-by-step guided experience
- Best for: Onboarding, help content

## Adding Audio

### Option 1: Background Music
- Select from Gruvi's library
- Or generate custom music
- Upbeat for engagement, calm for productivity apps

### Option 2: Voiceover
- Narrate the features
- Use Gruvi voiceover generation
- Script example:

```
Meet [App Name] - the easiest way to [main benefit].

Just tap here to [feature 1]...
And here to [feature 2]...

Download free today and see why thousands of users love it.
```

### Option 3: Music + Voiceover
Combine both for professional result.

## Best Practices

### For App Store Previews
- Keep under 30 seconds
- Show the most compelling 3-4 features
- Start with your best screen
- End with app icon and CTA

### For Social Media
- Shorter is better (15s ideal)
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Add captions for muted viewing

### For Ads
- Lead with the problem you solve
- Show solution quickly
- Clear CTA at end
- A/B test different openings

## Example Workflow

**Fitness App Launch**

1. **Screenshots captured:**
   - Workout selection screen
   - Exercise demo with timer
   - Progress tracking dashboard
   - Achievement/streak screen
   - Social sharing feature

2. **Generated prompt:**
   ```
   Create an energetic 20-second showcase of this fitness app,
   highlighting the workout variety, real-time tracking,
   and achievement system. Modern, motivating style.
   ```

3. **Audio selection:**
   - Generated upbeat electronic music
   - Added voiceover: "Your personal trainer, in your pocket..."

4. **Output:**
   - App Store preview (horizontal)
   - TikTok version (vertical)
   - Instagram Reel (square)

## Tips

1. **Best screenshots win** - Invest time in capturing great screens
2. **Tell a story** - User opens app → does thing → achieves goal
3. **Platform-specific** - Different cuts for different platforms
4. **Test on mobile** - Most viewers are on phones
5. **Sound off first** - Design for muted autoplay, then add audio
