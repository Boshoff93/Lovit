## What is Gruvi?

Gruvi is an AI-powered content creation platform that enables you to:

1. **Create AI Music** - Generate royalty-free songs in 32+ genres and 24 languages
2. **Create AI Videos** - Cinematic stories, music videos, UGC product demos
3. **Create AI Voiceovers** - Professional narration with 25+ voice personalities
4. **Character Swap** - Replace people in videos with AI characters
5. **Build AI Assets** - Create reusable characters, products, and locations
6. **Publish Everywhere** - Schedule and post to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and X
