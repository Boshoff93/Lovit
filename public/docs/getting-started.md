# Getting Started with Gruvi

Create your first AI content in under 5 minutes.

## 1. Create Your Account

1. Visit [agentgruvi.com](https://agentgruvi.com)
2. Click "Get Started"
3. Sign up with email or Google
4. Choose your subscription plan

## 2. Explore the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main creation options:

- **Create Music** - AI-generated songs
- **Create Voiceover** - Professional narration
- **Create Video** - Multiple video types
- **AI Assets** - Build reusable characters and products
- **Library** - Your created content
- **Schedule** - Plan and publish posts

## 3. Create Your First Song

1. Click **Create Music**
2. Enter a prompt describing your song:
   - Example: "An upbeat pop song about summer adventures, female vocals, energetic"
3. Select options:
   - **Mode**: Standard or Premium
   - **Length**: Short (30-90s) or Long (90-180s)
4. Click **Generate**
5. Wait ~60 seconds for your song

**Token cost**: 25-50 tokens depending on mode/length

## 4. Create Your First Video

1. Click **Create Video**
2. Choose a video type:
   - **Cinematic** - Story-driven visuals
   - **Music Video** - Visualize a song
   - **UGC Creator** - Product demo style
3. Select your song or create a voiceover
4. Customize style and settings
5. Click **Generate**

**Token cost**: 50 tokens per second of video

## 5. Publish to Social Media

1. Go to your **Library**
2. Select a video
3. Click **Schedule/Publish**
4. Choose platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, etc.)
5. Add metadata:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Hashtags
   - Thumbnail
6. Set schedule time or publish immediately

**Token cost**: 10 tokens for metadata generation
